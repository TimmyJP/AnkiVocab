# Privacy Policy for Anki Vocab Tool

**Effective Date:** June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Anki Vocab Tool" handles your data.

### 1. Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. We do not gather, store, or transmit any data that can be used to personally identify you.

### 2. Local Storage
The extension uses your browser's local storage solely to save your personal configuration settings (such as your preferred Anki Deck Name and Note Type). This data is stored exclusively on your local device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

### 3. Third-Party Services
* **Dictionary APIs:** To provide dictionary definitions and example sentences, the extension fetches data from [Jisho.org](https://jisho.org) and [Tatoeba.org](https://tatoeba.org) using their public APIs. 
* **AnkiConnect:** The extension communicates with the locally installed AnkiConnect add-on on your computer (via `http://127.0.0.1:8765`). This communication happens entirely on your local machine and does not involve any remote servers.

### 4. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository page.
